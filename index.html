About Me

Introduction

My name is Basak Erdem, and I am a Medical Office Administration student at Durham College in Oshawa, Ontario. With over 17 years of professional experience spanning education, administrative support, and client service across two countries, I bring a strong foundation in communication, organization, and problem-solving to the healthcare field.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in English Language Teaching from Gazi University in Ankara, Turkiye, and have since earned certifications in Microsoft Office (Word and PowerPoint), Google Project Management, and web design through Canva. I am proud to be on the Dean's Honour List for Fall 2025.

This ePortfolio showcases my mastery of the Essential Employability Skills developed throughout the Medical Office Administration program. Each section features a unique artifact from my coursework along with a reflection that highlights my learning, growth, and the impact on my professional future.
